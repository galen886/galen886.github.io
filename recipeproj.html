Breakfast!

Avocado Toast with Egg

Ingredients: 1 Slice whole grain bread, 1/2 avocado, 1 egg, salt, pepper

Instructions: Toast bread, mash avocado on top, cook egg (fried or poached), place on toast, season.

Banana Oat Pancakes

Ingredients: 1 banana, 2 eggs. 1/4 cup oats, pinch of cinnamon

Instructions: Blend all ingredients, cook small pancakes in a nonstick skillet over medium heat.

Greek Yogurt Parfait

Ingredients: 1 cup Greek yoguurt, 1/4 cup granola, 1/2 cup mixed berries, honey (optional)

Instructions: Layer yogury, berries, and granola. Drizzle honey on top.

Lunch!

Chicken Caesar Wrap

Ingredients: Tortilla wrap, cooked chicken breast, Caesar dressing, lettuce, Parmesan

Instructions: Toss chicken with dressing, wrap with lettuce and Parmesan in a Tortilla

Tuna Salad Sandwich

Ingredients: Canned tuna, mayo, diced celery, salt, pepper, bread

Instructions: Mix tuna, mayo, celery

Caprese Quesadilla

Ingredients: Mozzarella, tomato slices, basil, tortilla

Instructions: Layer ingerdients in tortilla, cook in pan until cheese melts and golden.

Dinner

Garlic Butter Shrimp Pasta

Ingredients: Spaghetti, shrimp, garlic, butter, parsley, lemon

Instructions: Cook pasta, saute shrimp in garlic butter, toss together with lemon and parsley.

One-Pan Chicken Fajitas

Ingredients: Chicken breast, bell peppers, onion, fajita seasoning

Instructions: Slice and saute everything in one pan until cooked through

Veggie Stir-Fry

Ingredients: Mixed veggies (fresh or frozen), soy sauce, garlic sesame oil, cooked rice

Instructions: Stir-fry veggies in sesame oil and garlic, add soy sauce, serve over rice

Dessert

Microwave Mug Brownie

Ingredients: 2 tbsp flour, 2 tbsp sugar, 1 tbsp cocoa powder, 2 tbsp milk, 1 tbsp oil

Instructions: Mix in mug, microwave for 1 minute

Chocolate-Dipped Strawberries

Ingredients: Strawberries, chocolate chips

Melt chocolate, dip strawberries, refrigerate to set.

No-Bake Peanut Butter Balls

Ingredients" 1 cup peanut butter, 1/2 cup honey, 1.5 cup oats

Instructions: Mix all, roll into balls, chill for 20 minutes
